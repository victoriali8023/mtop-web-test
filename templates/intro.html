Smartwatch Feedback Design Test

In this test, you will experience three scenarios. In each scenario, you need to read the task and finish it. Each scenario will be followed with a pop-up quiz to finish. After finishing three scenarios, you will need to answer and submit the post-questionnaire. At the end of the questionnaire, you will see the code.

This whole process will last about 15-20 mins.
