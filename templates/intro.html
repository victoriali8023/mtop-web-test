Smartwatch Feedback Design Study
  • Thank you for doing our study!
  • Please read all instructions carefully:
    • In this study, you will experience three scenarios.
    • In each scenario, you will be asked to play a session of an online Memory game and bring up the smartwatch interface, as described in the instructions at the start of the session.
    • Each session will be followed with a pop-up quiz.
    • After finishing all three sessions, you will need to complete a brief questionnaire.
    • When you complete the final questionnaire, you will be given a code (NOT your work ID!) which you will need to submit for this Amazon Mechanical Turk task to be approved.
  • Don't go back to the previous pages when you are not sure about something.
  • The whole task is intended to take about 10 minutes, but the taskit will remain open on MTurk for 20 minutes to account for short disruptions.
Task Background Information:
  • Recently, you have formed a team and donated to a charity. Your team has a daily goal for raising money so you are concerned about the progress.
  • Luckily, you could check this progress on the smartwatch you have just bought. You use it to check time, check activity progress, etc.
