Smartwatch Feedback Design Test

In this test, you will experience three scenarios. In each scenario, you need to read the task and finish it. Each scenario will be followed with a pop-up quiz to finish. After finishing three scenarios, you will need to answer and submit the post-questionnaire. At the end of the questionnaire, you will see the code which is needed to submit for Amazon Mechanical Turk.

Don't go back to the previous pages when you are not sure about something. The whole process will last about 15-20 mins.

Background:

Recently, you have formed a team and donated to a charity. Your team has a daily goal for raising money so you are concerned about the progress.

Luckily, you could check this progress on the smartwatch you have just bought. You use it to check time, check activity progress, etc.
